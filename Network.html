An IT network( Information Technology Network) refers to a group of interconnected devices that communicate with each other to share resources and data . These devices include: Computers Servers Printers Phones Networking equipment (routers and switches) Basic components of an IT network Nodes - Any devices connected to the network. For example ( computers, phones , servers etc) Router - Directs data between devices and the internet. Switch - Connects devices within the same local network( LAN) and passes data between them. Firewall - Protects the network from unauthorized acess or cyber threats. Acess Point - Allows wireless devices to contect to the network. Types of IT Networks Local Area Network (LAN): Connects devices in a limited area such as an office or home. Wide Area Network (WAN): Connects devices over a large geographical area such as across cities and countries. Metropolitan Area Network (MAN): Covers a larger area such as a city or large campus. Personal Area Network (PAN): Connects devices around an individual user such as a smart phone and a Bluetooth headset.
